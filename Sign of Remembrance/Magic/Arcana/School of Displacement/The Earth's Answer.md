* Casting time: 10 minutes
* Range: 100' sphere
* Duration: Until dispelled
* Exhausts by 1

_Question the earth,
That is strong and true;
Question the path,
Which you go through;
And soon it'll be seen,
A snap in the seam,
While the earth's answer swallows you whole._

You infuse the earth with the arcane field [[Arcane Field#The Earth's Answer|The Earth's Answer]]. Once unraveled, the field lasts for 1 hour.

**At Higher Levels:**
* (+1) The field's radius is increased by 50'.
* (+1) The field lasts for an hour longer once unraveled.
