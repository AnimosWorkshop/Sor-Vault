- Casting Time: Action
- Range: 10'
- Duration: 1 minute
- Exhausts by 1 when this spell ends

You tug on the strings of time to ebb and flow out of sync, allowing a creature to trade time between actions. Choose a creature you can sense within range. On their turn, the booned creature may use 2 reactions to use another quick action. In addition, they may use a quick action on their turn to reduce the DC of reaction checks until the start of their next turn by 5.

**At Higher Levels:** 
* (+1) You may target an additional creature.
