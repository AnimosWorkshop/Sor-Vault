#2nd-Level #Arcana #Calefaction
 
- Casting Time: 1 minute
- Range: Touch
- Duration: Instantaneous / Concentration, up to 4 minutes

You cool the air's humidity in an area near you into a drinkable water supply. Choose one of the following:
* Without any further requirements, the water is created in the air, and in the end of the casting time forms fully and then drops to the ground nearby. Up to 8 liters of water can be formed this way, and it can be made to be an approximately 3.5' cube.
* You may touch up to 16 open containers over the spell's casting time. The condensed water is formed nearby, then flows without disruption into the containers over the next round. This too can create up to 8 liters of water.
* You may touch a creature and attempt to flow vapor into them instead, requiring you to maintain [[Reading a Spell Entry#^01869f|concentration]] on the spell. They must succeed on a Resilience saving throw with disadvantage or start [[Conditions#Drowning|drowning]]. At the end of their turn, they may reattempt the saving throw without disadvantage, ending the spell upon succeeding.

**At Higher Levels:** 
* (+1) You may condense 2 more liters of water and over up to 4 additional containers.
