- Casting Time: Action
- Range: 10'
- Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute  
- Exhausts by 1 when this spell ends

You stimulate the nerves of a creature you can sense within range. For the boon's duration, the creature gains an additional action during their turn, which may be used to perform a dash, major interaction, or attack action. The target also gets a +1 bonus to their AC but a -1 penalty for saves for the boon's duration.
 
**At Higher Levels:** 
* (+1) You may target an additional creature.
