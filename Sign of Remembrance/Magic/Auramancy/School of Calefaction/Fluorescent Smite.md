#1st-Level #Auramancy #Calefaction #Curse
 
- Casting Time: As a part of an attack action, using a melee weapon
- Range: Self
- Duration: Until the start of your next turn  

You infuse your attack with flaring radiance, allowing the target to be seen better as the infused energy is reflected by the creature. On a hit, the attack deals 2d6 additional sensory damage, and the creature is cursed - making the next attack made against them have a +2 bonus to the attack roll, consuming the curse on a hit.

The infused attack is affected both by both weapon and spell focus potency modifiers if the weapon used is an auramantic spell focus.
 
**At Higher Levels:** 
* (+1) The attack deals additional 1d6 sensory damage.
