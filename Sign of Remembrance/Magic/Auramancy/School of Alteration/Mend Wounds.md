#1st-Level #Auramancy #Alteration #Boon
 
- Casting Time: Action
- Range: Touch
- Duration: Restbound

You mend the wounds of a creature within your reach, ensuring their safety. The creature restores hit points equal to 1d6 + your auramancy modifier. Any excess hit points restored turn into half as much temporary hit points, as the spell continues to protect them.
 
**At Higher Levels:** 
* (+1) The creature restores 6 additional hit points.
