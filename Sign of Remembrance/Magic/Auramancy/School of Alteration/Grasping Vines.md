#2nd-Level #Auramancy #Alteration #Boon
 
- Casting Time: Action
- Range: Self
- Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute
 
You modify your limbs to become stiffer and infused with mana. For the duration of the spell, you may make a brawl action as a part of casting the spell, and may make a [[Action Economy#^b70e3f|brawl action]] as a quick action for the duration of the spell.
