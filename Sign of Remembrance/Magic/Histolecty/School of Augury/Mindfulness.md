#3rd-Level #Histolecty #Augury
 
- Casting Time: 10 minutes
- Range: 40'
- Duration: Restbound

Your spirit becomes elevated, sensing and connecting with the creatures around you. For the duration of the spell, you are able to sense creatures within range, even if they are hidden behind objects, unless they are behind lead. This includes: lighting conditions, fog, invisibility, etc., and even underground up to 10' down.
If there are more than 20 small or larger creatures within range, the effects of the spell becomes overbearing, and you take a -2 penalty to Perception (Wisdom) checks and Resilience saves. You may disable the spell's effects, but it takes the spell's casting time to reactivate them.
 
**At Higher Levels:** 
* (+1) The spell can be cast twice as fast.
