- Casting Time: As a part of an attack action, using a melee weapon
- Range: Self
- Duration: Until the end of your next turn

You bring extra heft to a strike with a melee weapon, making your target susceptible to more incoming blows. If your attack hits, it deals 1d6 additional kinetic damage and the attacked creature must make a Wits saving throw. On a failed save, it becomes vulnerable 1 to sensory damage for this spell's duration. 
No matter the save's result, the creature then takes 1d6 sensory damage. If they are histolectually bound to you, raise this to 1d8.

The infused attack is affected both by both weapon and spell focus potency modifiers if the weapon used is a histolectual spell focus.

**At Higher Levels:**
* (3rd) The creature becomes vulnerable 2 on a failed save instead.
* (+1) Add an additional damage die to the sensory damage.
