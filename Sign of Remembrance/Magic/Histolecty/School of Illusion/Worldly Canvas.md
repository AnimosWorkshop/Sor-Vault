- Casting Time: 1 minute
- Range: Self
- Duration: Restbound

You use your histolectual bonds to manifest a physical vision of your imagination, able to make illusory throughout the world with your mind. You may make the drawing as fast as a reasonable person could draw on paper and erase at the same rate, and only and all creatures who are at a time histolectually bound to you can see the illusion.
Your bound creatures may make a Wits save to ignore or become aware again of your illusion. You, on the other hand, cannot unsee the illusion unless you erase it.
