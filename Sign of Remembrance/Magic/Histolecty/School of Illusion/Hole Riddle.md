#1st-Level #Histolecty #Illusion 

* Casting Time: As a part of an attack action, using a ranged weapon
* Range: Self
* Duration: Until the end of your next turn  

You enchant your weapon with a confusing spell, occupying the target's mind. On a hit, the attack deals 2d6 additional sensory damage, and the creature is cursed, giving them a -2 penalty to their next Wits save. If the target is histolectually bound to you, this penalty increases to -3.

The infused attack is affected both by both weapon and spell focus potency modifiers if the weapon used is a histolectual spell focus.

**At Higher Levels:** The attack deals additional 1d6 sensory damage for each spell level above 1st.
