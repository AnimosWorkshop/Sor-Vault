#Race #Undead
- - -
**Racial Attribute Increase:** Your Dexterity score decreases by 1, and your Wisdom score increases by 1.  
**Age:** Decay around 60 years after their undeath.  
**Size:** As you were in life.
**Creature Type:** Undead
**Speed:** As you had in life.
**Vision:** Nullvision, mistvision.
**Ghoul Fortitude:** You lose 8 maximum hit points and your natural armor's base AC is lowered by 1, but are resistant to kinetic damage.
**Undead Purpose:** Indahlings are usually victims of great crises, usually acts of terror or natural disasters, where many died in a short span of time. Being surrounded by these souls infuses the most Willing with those who were not, fracturing their outer mortal shell to accept others. The body of indahlings is fractured and chimera-looking, with foreign aura mixing into the familiar. Soon enough they struggle and become obsessed with the voices in their heads, aching for unity within the chaos. Each day, indahlings must give in to a different one of the souls within them, carrying their wishes and way of living. This cycle continues until a month passes, after which the indahling's own mind finally takes control of the body.
**Mental Looseness:** While suffering a negative condition, an [[Injuries|injury]] or a curse, you have a +1 bonus to saving throws.
**Languages:** You can speak, read, and write Peralian. This counts towards your proficiency cap.
