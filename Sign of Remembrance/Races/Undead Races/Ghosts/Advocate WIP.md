#Race #Undead
- - -
**Racial Attribute Increase:** Your Strength score decreases by 1, and your Intelligence score increases by 1.  
**Age:** Decay around 30 years after their undeath.  
**Size:** As you were in life
**Creature Type:** Undead
**Speed:** As you had in life.  
**Vision:** Nullvision, mistvision.
**Ghoul Fortitude:** You lose 8 maximum hit points and your natural armor's base AC is lowered by 1, but are resistant to kinetic damage.
**Undead Purpose:** Advocates are creatures who died due to their hubris and high self-importance, which eventually led to their own demise. As undead, this desire becomes an obsession, manifesting their strong spirit as a ghoul. In order for an advocate to complete their purpose, they must dedicate their being to fulfilling it until all options fail.
**:** 
**Languages:** You can speak, read, and write Peralian. This counts towards your proficiency cap.
